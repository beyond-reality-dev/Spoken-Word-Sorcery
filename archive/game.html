Spoken Word Sorcery

Spoken Word Sorcery is a game where you can create spells by speaking them out loud. The game will listen to your voice and convert it into a spell that you can use to defeat your enemies.

Click the button below to start the game.

Instructions

1. Click the "Start Game" button to begin.

2. Speak a spell out loud to cast it.

3. Use the spell to defeat your enemies.

4. Have fun!
